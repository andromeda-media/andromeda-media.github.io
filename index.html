      • Now, here are three different kinds of movies...

  • Live-Action

    • This is the name for movies that are made using real actors, hence the name "Live Action".

    The image above is from the live action film, Little Women

  • Animated

    • This is the name for movies that use animated drawn images, essentially cartoons. With technology today, we can use computers to synthesize animation or CGI however in the past it was made with flip books and hand drawn images.

    The image above shows one of the best pieces of animated media, Monsters inc.

  • Stop motion

    • Stop motion can be done through many medias. For instance, some stop motion films use plastic models, clay models, or even folded paper to serve as roles in the film. The models appear to have fluid motion because the individual pictures of the model at each position are seamlessly pasted together to create a movie scene.
